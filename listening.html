10100 Things You Said

10100 Things You Said is a speculative provocation – a room which listens.

Utilising speech recogniton in the browser, the web app represents the fleeting nature of speech, while logging the last 100 words spoken in the backend.

I intended for the project to evolve into an immersive installation with careful consideration to acoustics and different langauges.
